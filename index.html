Lyricity

This is Lyricity. My team and I created a web lyrics app that allows a user to search for a song that they want to know the lyrics to. If the song is in our database it will generate the lyrics and album cover if available. The user can then favorite that song to save it to their favorites. On the left hand side of the page the user will find a favorites button. When they click on this it will open their favorites tab allowing them to view and revisit their favorited songs or clear their favorites in case they are wanting a new list. If the song is not in our database the user will be greeted with an inspiring Kanye quote and Kanye picture.
